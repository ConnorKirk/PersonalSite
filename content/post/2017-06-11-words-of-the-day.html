I came across two great new words today (in the Economist - Thanks Emily). I’m not sure I’ll get the chance to use them in conversation any time soon..

  • Sophistry Complex arguments to support a point that are purposely misleading

  • Sinecure A position or status that offers finacial or other benefits for little or no work
